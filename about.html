Our Story

A tale of passion, community, and the magic of simple moments

How It All Began

We are three friends who share a deep love for Studio Ghibli and the feeling of comfort its stories bring. What started as late-night talks about our favorite films soon grew into a dream—to create a space where that same warmth, imagination, and friendship could come alive.

Our café is built to be more than just a place for coffee. Every detail, from the cozy corners to the playful drinks, is designed to feel like stepping into a Ghibli world. We want guests to pause, breathe, and discover a sense of wonder with every visit.

Most of all, our café is about connection. Just as Ghibli films bring people together, we hope our space can become a home for dreamers, friends, and anyone searching for a little everyday magic.

What We Believe In

Sustainability

Just like the forests and spirits in Ghibli films, we believe in protecting the world around us. Our café is built on small choices that make a big difference—using eco-friendly packaging, reducing waste, and supporting local suppliers. Every cup of coffee is crafted with care for both peope and the planet, because magic should never come at the cost of nature.

Community

We believe every cup should feel like magic. That’s why we use carefully selected beans, fresh ingredients, and recipes made with love. From the first sip to the last, our goal is to give you comfort, joy, and the unforgettable feeling of stepping into a Ghibli world.

Quality

Our café is more than a place to drink coffee—it’s a home for dreamers, friends, and Ghibli lovers. We created this space to share the warmth, magic, and imagination that inspired us. Here, every cup is a story, every corner feels like a scene from a film, and everyone is part of the family. Welcome to our little world—you belong here.
